Mandvi Beach


The Mandvi beach is a stretch of golden-brown sand located at the southernmost point of Gujarat's Kutch district. It is located south of the main intersection, Bhuj. It is known for its tranquil atmosphere, picturesque sunsets, and range of activities, making it a popular destination .


The beach is known for its blue waters, soft white sands, and serene environment, making it a perfect spot for relaxation and family outings. The town has a rich maritime history, once serving as a centre for shipbuilding, with skilled Kharva craftsmen who played a significant role in maritime trade.


It is popular for its picturesque sunsets and tranquil atmosphere, which draw visitors looking for a peaceful beach experience away from the hustle and bustle of city life.

General information

Mandvi Beach is celebrated for its expansive shoreline that stretches with crystal-clear waters and soft sandy beaches.

What to eat

Must try Kutchi Dabeli, which was invented in Mandvi. You can also enjoy a Gujarati thali, street snacks like khichdi-kadhi.

Best time to visit

The cooler months, from October to March, are the best time to visit Mandvi Beach. The evenings are particularly popular for watching the sunset.

Attractions

There are numerous water activities available here, it is also the ideal location for adventure seekers. You may also go horseback riding or camel riding.
